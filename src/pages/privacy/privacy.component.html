Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024-07-20

1. Introduction

SCPXY ("we", "our", "us") values your privacy above all else. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, what we do not collect, and how we handle your data when you use our VPN software and website.

By using SCPXY, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Our "No-Logs" Guarantee (Activity Data)

To protect your privacy, we do not log your browsing activity. When you are connected to our VPN, we do NOT collect, store, or monitor:

  • The websites you visit.
  • The content of your data traffic.
  • DNS queries.
  • The IP addresses of the websites you access.

Because we do not collect this data, we cannot provide it to third parties or law enforcement, even if compelled to do so.

3. Information We DO Collect

To provide our service, process payments, and enforce our Terms of Service (specifically the refund policy), we collect the following limited information:

A. Account Information

  • Email Address: Required to create an account, manage your subscription, and reset passwords.
  • Password: Stored in an encrypted (hashed) format. We cannot see your actual password.

B. Payment Information

  • Transaction Data: When you make a purchase, our third-party payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) handle your financial data. We do not store your full credit card details. We only retain a transaction ID and the subscription status to verify your payment.

C. Technical & Usage Data (Minimal Logging)

To enforce our Refund Policy and ensure fair usage, we collect minimal technical statistics:

  • Total Bandwidth Consumed: We track the amount of data transferred (in MB/GB) to determine if you have used the service. This allows us to verify eligibility for the "No Traffic Used" refund condition.
  • Account Status: Whether your account is active or suspended.

Note: This technical data is not tied to specific websites you visit. It is simply a counter of data volume.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To authenticate your account and establish the VPN connection.
  • Billing: To process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • Support: To assist you with technical issues or refund requests.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with New Zealand laws and regulations (e.g., tax records).

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Location: Our primary operations are based in New Zealand.
  • Security Measures: We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) to protect your data in transit and at rest. Access to user databases is strictly limited to authorized personnel.
  • Data Retention: We retain account and billing information for as long as your account is active or as required by tax laws. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data, retaining only what is necessary for legal or accounting obligations.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to operate our business (e.g., payment processors, email delivery services). These parties are authorized to use your personal information only as necessary to provide these services to us.

7. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may disclose your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by New Zealand law or a valid court order. However, since we do not store browsing history, we cannot disclose browsing activity.
  • Business Transfers: If SCPXY is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your account information may be transferred.

8. Your Rights (NZ Privacy Act 2020)

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Access: Ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct any wrong information we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and personal data (subject to legal retention requirements).

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
